Welcome to Café au Lait

What is Café au Lait?

Our employees are the creme of the crop, being trained for years in the art of service. This experience has given them great skill in the art of serving. We are precise in meeting the demands of are customers to the fullest extent. This is a title we hold with great pride and respect as we try our best to uphold the great expectations on our shoulders of those who have helped us become what we are today.
