Café au Lait is our café's speciallty and number one best selling product. Made with 50% milk and 50% black coffee this drink is the best known for being a perfect balance of light and dark. Vantablack Coffee is a coffee like none other. Named after the recently discovered artificial material, Vantablack which is known for being the darkest man-made substance due to the fact it absorbs 99.96% of all light that hits it's surface. The coffee beans used in our coffee were named after the material after being found growing in a cave in the south tropics being almost completely void of sunlight. These beans were found to absorb almost all light like Vantablack but with one added effect. One cup of this coffee packs enough caffeine to stay up 3 days and nights! That's why Vantablack Coffee is popular among students needing to finish a last minute essay and businessmen who want to finish their paper work in time for the holidays. However, we worn this coffee is not for everyone because after those 3 days of energy follows three days of coma like sleep to make up for it. Will you dare to drink the drink known as the Devil's Coffee? The choice is yours!
